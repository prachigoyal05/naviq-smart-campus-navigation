NAV IQ: Ground Floor Knowledge Base This document contains all location data and routing information for the ground floor, based on the official campus map. It is used as the primary data source for the NAV IQ AI Assistant. ________________________________________ 📍 Locations and Descriptions All descriptions are based on spatial relationships shown on the map. Central Area ●	ATRIUM: The main, large central hub of the building. It connects the left wing (Library/Admin) to the right wing (Pits Cafe/Auditorium). It is accessible from the central bottom ENTRY. ●	ENTRY (Center): The main entrance at the bottom of the map, leading directly into the ATRIUM. Left Wing (Academic & Hostels) ●	GIRLS HOSTEL: Located in the top-left corner of the building. ●	LOUNGE: A common area situated between the GIRLS HOSTEL and the labs (CL 1, CL 2). ●	CL 1 & CL 2: A set of computer labs located near the LOUNGE and the BOYS HOSTEL. ●	BOYS HOSTEL: Located in the left wing, near the CL labs and the AUTOCAD LAB. ●	AUTOCAD LAB: A specialized lab near seminar hall towards girls hostel ●	LAB: A general lab situated between the AUTOCAD LAB and the SEMINAR HALL. ●	SEMINAR HALL: Located in the left wing, positioned between the LAB and the LIBRARY. ●	LIBRARY: A large facility in the left wing, accessible from the ATRIUM. It is located next to the SEMINAR HALL and the ADMIN OFFICE. ●	ADMIN OFFICE: The administrative office, located in the bottom-left corner of the building, next to the LIBRARY and near the left-side ENTRY. ●	ENTRY (Left): An entrance on the bottom-left of the building, providing access near the ADMIN OFFICE and LIBRARY. Right Wing (Facilities & Services) ●	CAFETERIA: The main food area, located in the top-right section of the building. ●	TUCS: An office or store located in the top-right corner, directly adjacent to the CAFETERIA and the ADMISSION OFFICE. ●	ADMISSION OFFICE: Located in the top-right, next to TUCS and near the right-side ENTRY. ●	PITS CAFE: A smaller cafe located on the edge of the ATRIUM, leading towards the AUDITORIUM. ●	AUDITORIUM: The main auditorium for events, located in the right wing. It is next to the PITS CAFE and a WASHROOM. ●	MDP ROOM: A room located in the bottom-right corner of the building. ●	LIFT & STAIRS: Located in the bottom-right area, providing vertical access. They are next to each other and near the MDP ROOM. ●	ENTRY (Right): An entrance on the far-right side of the building, providing access near the ADMISSION OFFICE and a WASHROOM. ●	WASHROOM (x3): There are three washrooms on this floor: 1.	One is located next to the CAFETERIA. 2.	One is located between the ATRIUM and the AUDITORIUM. 3.	One is located near the right-side ENTRY, close to the ADMISSION OFFICE. ________________________________________ 🤖 AI Assistant FAQs General Location Questions: ●	Q: Where is the Cafeteria? A: The Cafeteria is in the top-right area of the building, next to TUCS. ●	Q: Where can I find the Admission Office? A: The Admission Office is in the top-right corner, next to TUCS and near the right-side entry. ●	Q: Where is the Library? A: The Library is in the left wing, next to the Seminar Hall. You can get to it from the central ATRIUM. ●	Q: I'm looking for the Auditorium. A: The Auditorium is in the right wing. From the central ATRIUM, go past the PITS CAFE, and the Auditorium is right there. ●	Q: Where is the Admin Office? A: The Admin Office is in the bottom-left corner of the building, next to the Library and the left-side entry. ●	Q: Where are the washrooms? A: There are three washrooms: one next to the CAFETERIA, one near the AUDITORIUM, and one near the right-side ENTRY by the Admission Office. ●	Q: Where are the hostels? A: The Girls Hostel is in the top-left corner. The Boys Hostel is also in the left wing, near the AUTOCAD LAB and CL labs. Navigation & Routing Questions: ●	Q: How do I get to the Library from the main Atrium? A: From the ATRIUM, head to the left wing. The Library is the large room on your right, before the Admin Office. ●	Q: What is the path to the Auditorium? A: Enter the main ATRIUM and walk towards the right. Go past the PITS CAFE, and the AUDITORIUM is directly ahead. ●	Q: I'm at the Admission Office. How do I get to the Cafeteria? A: They are right next to each other. From the Admission Office, walk past TUCS, and you will be at the Cafeteria. ●	Q: How do I find the Seminar Hall? A: Go to the left wing, past the Library. The Seminar Hall is located between the Library and the LAB. ●	Q: Where is the lift? A: The LIFT and STAIRS are located together in the bottom-right of the building, next to the MDP ROOM. Facility Questions: ●	Q: Where can I get food or coffee? A: You can go to the main CAFETERIA in the top-right, or to the PITS CAFE, which is near the ATRIUM and AUDITORIUM. ●	Q: Is there a lounge area? A: Yes, there is a LOUNGE in the top-left, located between the Girls Hostel and the CL labs. ●	Q: Where are the computer labs? A: The main computer labs (CL 1, CL 2) are in the left wing near the hostels. There is also an AUTOCAD LAB and another LAB in the same area, near the Seminar Hall.
